Pokemon Cries - written in HTML/CSS/Javascript

About Pokemon Cries

The player must listen to a Pokemon's cry and try to identify which Pokemon it is. Answering correctly unlocks its shiny on its next appearance(s). Guessing incorrectly reduces your lives by 1. Be careful! You only have 8.

After losing all your lives you get to see which shinies you have unlocked and you can also see your collection. Collect them all!

Triple Triad - written in C++

About Triple Triad

Final Fantasy VIII's Triple Triad but with Pokemon. Each player starts with 5 cards that contain different numbers from 0 to 9(+A) in each direction (up, down, left, right). Each player takes turns placing a card in the 3x3 grid.

After placing a card: if the number on the adjecent card(s) is lower than the number on the played card, the adjecent card(s) gets flipped over. After all 9 squares are filled, the player with the most cards flipped to their colour wins.
